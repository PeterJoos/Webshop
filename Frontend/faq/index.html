  • Account

  • How do I sign up?
    1. Go to Fit Labs
    2. In the top right, click Sign in.
    3. Click Create Account.
  • How do I change my password?
    1. Log into your Fit Labs account
    2. Click the personal icon in the top-right of your screen, then click Settings
    3. Click Account settings from the navigation
    4. Click Change your password
    5. Enter your old password followed by your new password
      • If you cannot remember your old password, click Forgot it? and check the email associated with your Pinterest account to reset your password
    6. Click Change Password
    7. Click Done
  • How do I change my account settings?
    1. Log into your Fit Labs account
    2. Click the personal icon in the top-right of your screen, then click Settings
    3. Click Account settings from the navigation
    4. For your address, email address and phone number start by clicking the + sign to add or edit your information. You can select your language from the language dropdown on the left side of the page.
  • I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
    1. Click on the Forgot Password link in the Fit Labs login screen. Enter your registered email address.
    2. You will receive an email to the registered email address with the password reset link. Click the link and reset the password.
    3. You will now be able to login to your Fit Labs account with your new password.

  • Payments

  • What forms of payment can I use?

    We accept PayPal payments, Back transfers, Mastercard, Visa or Western Union.

  • How will I know that my payment has been accepted?

    After you submit your payment, you will see a payment confirmation screen. It will contain your payment confirmation message. It will show an approved number for credit cards or a processed number for electronic check. You will also receive a confirmation email after your transaction is submitted. The email will include your account number, invoice number, amount paid, and confirmation message.

  • How long does it take for a credit card transaction to process if I pay online?

    Credit card transactions typically take 48 hours to settle. An authorization is issued immediately; however, it takes 48 hours for the money to be moved.

  • I have cancelled my order. How long will it take until I receive my refund?

    As soon as the store has registered your cancellation or your return, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

  • Is my payment information safe?

    Payment information is processed securely by Klarna. No card details are transferred to or held by Fit Labs. All transactions take place via connections secured with the latest industry standard security protocols.

  • Delivery

  • What should I do if my order hasn't been delivered yet?

    Your estimated delivery date is in your Order Confirmation email – please allow until this date for your order to arrive. You’ll be able to follow your order if it’s been sent with a trackable service. To check this, all you need to do is log into ‘My Account’ and view your order. If the status shows ‘Track This Order’, just click the link and you’ll be able to follow your parcel. If the status shows ‘Shipped’, your order has been sent with your local postal service and isn’t trackable. Your tracking link will be able to provide up to date information on the status of your order.If your estimated delivery date has passed and you haven’t received your order, just click here(link naar contact us?) so we can help you further.

  • What delivery methods are available?
    • We offer Standard, Express and Next-Day delivery services to most European countries.
    • Once you've entered your delivery address, you'll be able to see the delivery services available.*
    • Most of our delivery service are trackable. You'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking link once your order has left the warehouse.
    • If you’ve placed multiple orders on the same day using the same delivery method, your orders may be shipped in one parcel. If the tracking link in your shipping confirmation emails is the same, your orders have been sent with one of our delivery partners and will be arriving at the same time. You’ll be refunded for any additional delivery charges, you’ve paid for if we have combined your orders.
  • How do returns or refunds work?

    Our Standard 30 day online return options apply to goods purchased for domestic use only. This option do not apply to any Nutritional Supplements. Mail Order Returns/Ex. Display Models are classed as ‘one off’ ‘special order’ items, and as such are not covered by our standard 30 day online return option.


    Please note: our couriers do not collect any unpackaged goods.

  • How does your Next Day Delivery service work?

    You can select our next day delivery service at checkout and if your order is placed before 6pm your parcel will be delivered the next day before 8 pm. We deliver to residential addresses 7 days a week, except Bank holidays. We deliver to Pick-up Points Mon-Sat, except Bank holidays. Orders placed after the cut off will be delivered within 2 days.

  • When will my order arrive?

    You can find tracking information in your order details. If an order includes multiple items, each may have separate delivery dates and tracking information.

  • When will my order ship?

    Orders ordered before 6pm will be shipped the same day.

    Orders ordered after 6pm will be shipped the next day.
